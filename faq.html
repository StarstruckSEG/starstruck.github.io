How to connect your Spotify directly to Starstruck?

When you are logged into your account, go to Settings/Connect spotify and click on the hyperlink; you must accept the privacy policy and your account will be connected afterwards.

How Starstruck makes money?

Starstruck is completely free for users. We make money from advertising directly in the website.

What should I do if I have lost my account password?

You can call the Starstruck hotline anytime at (645) 7689-9876. You can also click on the "I forgot my password" link on the "create an account" page.

Am I able to connect Apple Music with Starstruck?

Currently, the software only allows with Spotify. However, it will soon be possible with Apple Music!
