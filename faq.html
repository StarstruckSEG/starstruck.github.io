FAQ'S

How to connect your Spotify directly to Startruck?

When you are logged into your account, go to Settings/Connect spotify and click on the hyperlink; you must accept the privacy policy and your account will be connected afterwards.

How Startruck makes money?

The user pays a monthly subscription when he creates an account with Startruck

What should I do if I have lost my account password?

You can call the Startruck hotline anytime at (645) 7689-9876. You can also click on the "I forgot my password" link on the "create an account" page.
